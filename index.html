About the firm

ME Geospatial Services grew out of a book. ReBuilding Liberia: Infrastructure Strategy, Human Capital, and the Road to Prosperity. It’s what brought Matthew Elious out of retirement to promote critical Infrastructure development in Liberia and other developing countries.

ME Geospatial Services LLC is a senior-practitioner advisory and implementation firm working at the intersection of geodetic infrastructure, geospatial data systems, and institutional capacity. Its current flagship work is in Liberia. Its services apply equally to the United States, where decades of State DOT production and proposal experience translate directly into prime-contractor support.

What we do

  • Design and operate geospatial data infrastructure web portals
  • Help build and densify geodetic reference frameworks
  • Plan and process drone imagery and LiDAR acquisitions
  • Develop scopes of work, technical specs, and work plans for geospatial projects

What we do not do

  • Generic IT services
  • Web design for other clients

Services

Four lines of work.

Geospatial Data Systems & Portals

Design and operation of web-native, federated geospatial portals — the pattern proven in LrOneMap — together with base map review and update.

Geodetic Reference Frameworks

Reference-network design and densification, geoid determination, and standards. Author of the Liberia Geodetic Reference Framework (LGREF).

Photogrammetric & LiDAR Data Production

Processing of drone and UAV imagery and LiDAR, with vector and digital terrain model (DTM) extraction, through established office and subcontract capacity.

U.S. & West Africa

Project & Production Advisory

Scope-of-work development, technical specifications, and work plans for geospatial projects. Independent quality review of in-progress and completed projects, on call to prime firms and agencies. Proposal support — built on senior State DOT project-management experience.
